PersianLUG

Persian LUG

Persian LUG is a non-profit organization providing support and educational opportunities for Persian Linux community.

We organize meetups, workshops, and other events to help Persian Linux users learn more about Linux and open source technologies. We also provide online support and resources to help users troubleshoot any problems they may have.

Events

Participation in the meetings is open and free for all those interested based on capacity and conditions. Each session will include an open discussion, a news review, and a related presentation, which is voluntary. Persianlog meetings are held in Persian.
